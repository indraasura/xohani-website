Agile training services

Unlock Your Team's Potential with Agile Training

At Xohani Solutions, we believe that agility is the key to thriving in today’s fast-paced business environment. Our Agile Training Services are designed to equip your team with the knowledge and skills necessary to implement Agile methodologies effectively, fostering a culture of collaboration, flexibility, and continuous improvement.

    Benefits of agile training with Xohani Solutions

  • Tailored Training Programs: Our training sessions are customized to meet the specific needs and maturity levels of your teams

  • Hands-on Learning: We emphasize practical, hands-on exercises and real-world scenarios to reinforce learning and application.

  • Expert Instructors: Our trainers are experienced Agile practitioners with a wealth of knowledge and insights to share.

  • Enhanced Team Collaboration: Foster a collaborative environment that encourages innovation and responsiveness to change.

Here's what you'll learn with us

Our Agile Training Package

Agile Fundamentals
  • Introduction to Agile: Understand the core principles and values of Agile methodologies, including the Agile Manifesto.

  • Agile frameworks' overview: Explore various Agile frameworks such as Scrum, Kanban, and Lean, and learn how to choose the right one for your organization.

  • Roles and responsibilites: Gain insights into the key roles within Agile teams, including Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Development Team members.

Scrum Master Training​
  • Scrum Framework Deep Dive: Learn the intricacies of the Scrum framework, including sprints, ceremonies, and artifacts.

  • Facilitation Skills: Develop essential facilitation skills to guide teams through Scrum events and foster collaboration.

  • Conflict Resolution: Equip yourself with techniques to manage conflicts and challenges within Agile teams effectively.

Product Owner Training​
  • Defining the Product Vision: Learn how to create and communicate a compelling product vision that aligns with business goals.

  • Backlog Management: Master the art of prioritizing and managing the product backlog to maximize value delivery.

  • Stakeholder Engagement: Understand how to effectively engage with stakeholders to gather requirements and feedback.
