Salesforce services

At Xohani Solutions, we are passionate about helping businesses optimize their operations with Salesforce. Our team of certified experts specializes in delivering tailored ServiceNow solutions across various modules, including:

Salesforce sales cloud
  • Lead Management: Capture, track, and nurture leads effectively to convert them into loyal customers.

  • Opportunity Management: Streamline the sales process by managing opportunities from initial contact to closing deals.

  • Sales Forecasting: Utilize advanced analytics to predict sales trends and make informed business decisions.

  • Collaboration Tools: Enhance team collaboration with integrated tools that facilitate communication and information sharing.

Salesforce service cloud
  • Case Management: Efficiently manage customer inquiries and issues to ensure timely resolutions and high satisfaction.

  • Knowledge Base: Create a centralized repository of information to empower customers and support agents with self-service options.

  • Omni-Channel Support: Provide seamless support across multiple channels, including phone, email, chat, and social media.

Salesforce marketing cloud
  • Email Marketing: Design and automate personalized email campaigns to engage your audience and drive conversions.

  • Social Media Marketing: Manage and analyze social media campaigns to enhance brand visibility and customer engagement.

  • Customer Journey Mapping: Create tailored customer journeys to deliver the right message at the right time.

  • Analytics and Reporting: Gain insights into campaign performance and customer behavior to optimize marketing strategies.

Salesforce community cloud
  • Customer Communities: Build online communities for customers to connect, share experiences, and access resources.

  • Partner Portals: Create secure portals for partners to collaborate, share information, and manage relationships.

  • Employee Engagement: Foster a collaborative environment for employees to share knowledge and resources.
